Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you visit or interact with this website and its humanitarian and philanthropic initiatives. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy while maintaining transparency in how personal information is handled across all our services and outreach activities.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you interact with our website, including your name, email address, phone number, and donation-related details. This information may be submitted through contact forms, newsletter subscriptions, event registrations, or donation platforms.

In addition, certain non-personal information such as browser type, device information, IP address, and website usage data may be automatically collected to help improve website performance and user experience.

How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely to support our humanitarian and philanthropic activities. This includes communicating with supporters, responding to inquiries, processing donations, improving website functionality, and sharing updates about outreach programs and community initiatives.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared with trusted service providers when necessary to operate the website, process donations securely, or deliver services aligned with our mission.

Your Choices

You have the right to request access to your personal information and to ask for corrections or removal where applicable. Users may also choose to opt out of receiving communications or updates at any time by following unsubscribe instructions or contacting us directly.

We respect your control over your personal data and aim to provide clear options regarding how your information is used.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information is managed, please reach out through the contact page on this website. We are committed to protecting your privacy while continuing to serve communities through responsible and transparent humanitarian work.
